Kakapo Cabin

Kakapo Snow Mountain Cabins are nestled in the heart of a picturesque mountain range. The cabins offer a cozy and rustic retreat for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers. Inside the cabins, you'll find a warm and inviting atmosphere with comfortable furnishings and modern amenities. The living area features a fireplace, perfect for snuggling up with a book or enjoying a hot cup of cocoa. The fully equipped kitchen allows you to prepare your own meals, or you can dine at the nearby restaurant that offers delicious local cuisine. The bedrooms are spacious and offer breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains. Outside, you'll find a private deck where you can relax and take in the fresh mountain air. The surrounding area is a paradise for outdoor activities, with hiking trails, skiing slopes, and scenic spots just a stone's throw away. Whether you're looking for a peaceful getaway or an action-packed adventure, Kakapo Snow Mountain Cabins provide the perfect base for your mountain retreat.

Features

  • Maximum Adults: 10
  • Maximum Children: 30
  • Largest of the Chalets
  • Large kitchen, dining and living area
  • Great amenities

Kereru Cabin

Kereru Snow Mountain Cabins offer a truly enchanting escape in the midst of a snow-covered wonderland. Step inside these cozy cabins and you'll be greeted by a rustic yet inviting atmosphere. The interior is adorned with charming wooden accents and comfortable furnishings, creating a warm and welcoming ambiance. The living area features a crackling fireplace, perfect for curling up with a book or enjoying intimate conversations with loved ones. The fully equipped kitchen allows you to prepare delicious meals, while the bedrooms offer a peaceful retreat with breathtaking views of the snow-dusted landscape. Surround yourself with the beauty of nature and indulge in a range of outdoor activities, from skiing and snowboarding to snowshoeing and winter hikes. Whether you're seeking adventure or relaxation, Kereru Snow Mountain Cabins provide the perfect sanctuary for your snowy mountain getaway.

Features

  • Maximum Adults: 2
  • Maximum Children: 4
  • Great option for families
  • 2 bedrooms
  • Seperate Living Areas
